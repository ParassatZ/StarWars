Planets




Naboo

A planet in the Chommell sector near the Outer Rim. It is inhabited by two independent societies — the Gungans, who live in underwater cities, and the Nabuans, people who inhabit the surface of the planet. The homeworld of Padme Amidala Naberry and Ja-Ja Binks, as well as Senator and future Emperor Palpatine.

Region: Mid Rim Territories

Sector: Chommell

Moons: Ohma-D'un, Onoam, Veruna

Climate: Temperate, breathable, Swamps, Hills, Plains, Cities, Mountains

Fauna: Bee, Blarth, Bogwing, Colo claw fish, Doo

Coruscant

Since the dawn of interstellar history, the single word "Coruscant" has concealed the undisputed center of power, strength, legality, history and culture.

Region: Core Worlds

Sector: Corusca

Moons: Centax-1, Centax-2, Centax-3, Hesperidium

Climate: Temperate, breathable

Fauna: Conduit worm, Crown finch, Duracrete slug, Granite slug, Pikobi

Tatooine

An Outer Ring planet located at the far edge of the Galaxy. It is located at the intersection of many hyperspace routes, and therefore many merchants use the Mos Eisley spaceport as a transfer point. Far from the watchful eyes of the Galactic Empire, Tatooine has become a haven for various kinds of adventurers — smugglers, mercenaries, bounty hunters.

Region: Outer Rim Territories

Sector: Arkanis

Moons: Ghomrassen, Guermessa, Chenini

Climate: Hot and arid, breathable, Canyons, Desert, Mountains, Rocky bluffs

Fauna: Bantha, Canyon krayt dragon, Cave sketto, Dewback, Dune lizard
